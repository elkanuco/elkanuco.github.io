PRINCE2 Overview

PRINCE2 (PRojects IN Controlled Environments) is a process-based project management method that provides a structured framework for managing projects of any size or complexity.

The 7th Edition introduces refined practices, enhanced management approaches, and updated guidance for modern project delivery including sustainability, digital transformation, and people-centric leadership.

What is PRINCE2?

PRINCE2 is a widely adopted project management methodology used globally across industries and sectors. It provides:

  • A controlled start, middle, and end to projects
  • Regular reviews of progress against plan and business case
  • Flexible decision points for project continuation
  • Automatic management control of deviations from plan
  • Involvement of stakeholders at appropriate times

The Five Elements of PRINCE2

PRINCE2 7th Edition is built on five foundational elements that work together:

Element Description Purpose
Principles Seven universal truths that guide decision-making Provide consistent guidance and shape methodology application
People Human aspects of project management including leadership, communication, and collaboration Ensure effective teamwork and stakeholder engagement
Practices Seven knowledge areas covering specific project management topics Provide expertise on Business Case, Risk, Quality, Planning, and other areas
Processes Seven sequential activities through the project lifecycle Create a roadmap from project start-up to closure
Project Context Environmental and organizational considerations Enable tailoring to specific project circumstances

The Seven Principles

PRINCE2 principles are universal best practices that must always be applied:

Principle Description
1. Continued Business Justification Projects must have clear justification that remains valid throughout lifecycle
2. Learn from Experience Lessons are sought, recorded, and acted upon throughout project
3. Defined Roles and Responsibilities Clear project organization with defined accountability
4. Manage by Stages Projects are planned, monitored, and controlled stage-by-stage
5. Manage by Exception Delegated tolerances at each level enable effective control without micromanagement
6. Focus on Products Product-based planning ensures clarity about deliverables and quality
7. Tailor to Suit Environment PRINCE2 is adapted to project size, complexity, risk, and organizational context

The Seven Performance Variables

PRINCE2 requires monitoring seven key variables throughout the project lifecycle:

Variable Monitoring Focus
Time Schedule adherence and delivery dates
Cost Budget consumption and forecast costs
Quality Product conformance to specifications
Scope Agreed deliverables and boundaries
Risk Uncertainty exposure and threat/opportunity management
Benefits Expected outcomes and value realization
Sustainability Environmental, social, and economic long-term impact (NEW in 7th Edition)

Why Use PRINCE2?

Benefits

  • Controlled delivery: Systematic approach reduces risk of project failure
  • Clear accountability: Defined roles prevent confusion and gaps
  • Business focus: Ensures projects deliver value aligned with strategy
  • Scalability: Works for projects of any size through tailoring
  • Best practice: Incorporates decades of project management wisdom
  • Recognition: Global standard understood by employers and practitioners
  • Flexibility: Can be combined with Agile, Lean, and other approaches

When to Use PRINCE2

  • Complex projects requiring formal governance
  • Projects with significant investment or risk
  • Multi-stakeholder environments
  • Regulatory or compliance-driven projects
  • Projects requiring clear accountability and reporting
  • Organizations seeking standardized project delivery

PRINCE2 7th Edition Updates

Key Changes from Previous Versions

Aspect Change
Terminology "Themes" renamed to "Practices" for clarity
People Focus New emphasis on leadership, collaboration, and team dynamics
Sustainability Added as 7th performance variable; Sustainability Management Approach introduced
Digital & Data Digital and Data Management Approach added
Issue Types Expanded from 3 to 5 types (added Business Opportunity and External Event)
Product Register New management product for tracking all deliverables
Agile Integration Better guidance on combining PRINCE2 with Agile approaches

Getting Started with PRINCE2

Learning Path

  1. Understand the Principles - Core philosophy that never changes
  2. Learn the Practices - Knowledge areas for each project aspect
  3. Study the Processes - Step-by-step project lifecycle activities
  4. Explore Management Products - Documents and records used throughout
  5. Apply Tailoring - Adapt PRINCE2 to your project context

Certification Levels

Level Focus Target Audience
PRINCE2 Foundation Understanding of method and terminology Team members, aspiring project managers
PRINCE2 Practitioner Application and tailoring in real projects Project managers, project board members
PRINCE2 Agile Foundation Combining PRINCE2 with Agile delivery Agile teams using PRINCE2 governance
PRINCE2 Agile Practitioner Advanced integration of PRINCE2 and Agile Senior project managers in Agile environments
