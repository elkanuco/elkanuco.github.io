❤️ Scrum Values

Successful Scrum depends on living these values: Commitment, Focus, Openness, Respect, Courage.

Commitment

Commit to achieving goals and supporting each other.

Focus

Focus on Sprint work to make best progress toward goals.

Openness

Be open about work and challenges.

Respect

Respect each other as capable, independent people.

Courage

Have courage to do the right thing and tackle tough problems.
