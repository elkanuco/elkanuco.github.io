Implementation Tips

  • Use all ceremonies for at least two Sprints, then adjust via retrospectives.
  • Keep teams small (5-9 members) and cross-functional.
  • Prioritize user stories and maintain a dynamic Product Backlog.
  • Foster daily collaboration and clear communication.
  • Track velocity for forecasting, but rely on empiricism.

Common Challenges

  • Resistance to change: Educate stakeholders on benefits.
  • Difficulty prioritizing: Use value-based prioritization.
  • Overloaded Sprints: Focus on sustainable pace.
