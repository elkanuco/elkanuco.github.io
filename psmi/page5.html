The Sprint

Fixed-length event (1 month or less) where the team creates an Increment. No changes endanger the Sprint Goal.

Sprint Planning

Team plans the work for the Sprint, selects items from Product Backlog, and defines the Sprint Goal. Time-box: 8 hours for a 1-month Sprint.

Daily Scrum

15-minute daily meeting for Developers to synchronize and plan next 24 hours. Answers: What did I do? What will I do? Any impediments?

Sprint Review

Inspect the Increment and adapt Product Backlog. Stakeholders provide feedback. Time-box: 4 hours for a 1-month Sprint.

Sprint Retrospective

Team reflects on what went well and how to improve. Time-box: 3 hours for a 1-month Sprint.
