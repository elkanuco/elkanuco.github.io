The Scrum Team

The Scrum Team is small (typically 10 or fewer), cross-functional, and self-managing. It consists of one Product Owner, one Scrum Master, and Developers.

Product Owner

Accountable for maximizing product value. Manages the Product Backlog, defines the Product Goal, and ensures transparency.

Scrum Master

Accountable for Scrum adoption and team effectiveness. Coaches the team, removes impediments, and facilitates events.

Developers

Committed to creating a usable Increment each Sprint. They plan the Sprint Backlog, adhere to Definition of Done, and adapt daily.
