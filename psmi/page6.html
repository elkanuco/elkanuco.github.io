Product Backlog

Ordered list of everything known to be needed in the product. Managed by Product Owner; evolves with new information.

Sprint Backlog

Plan for delivering selected Product Backlog items during the Sprint. Includes Sprint Goal and tasks.

Increment

Sum of all Product Backlog items completed during a Sprint and prior Sprints. Must be "Done" and usable.

Commitment: Product Goal

Long-term objective for the product. Provides context for decision-making.
